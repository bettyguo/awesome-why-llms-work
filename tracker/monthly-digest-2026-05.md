## How to read future digests

Each future month will have:

- Top-N most-cited new papers per programme.
- Any status changes during the month.
- A short editorial paragraph naming the most consequential paper of the month
  *for the repo's evidence ledger*, not the most-shared paper.

Following [`reading-paths/30-minute-overview.md`](../reading-paths/30-minute-overview.md)
each month is a reasonable cadence: read the digest, skim the changed
programmes' ledgers, and pick up the most consequential paper for full reading.
